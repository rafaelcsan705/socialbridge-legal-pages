Data Deletion Instructions

GDPR Compliant

Step 1: Revoke App Access

  1. Go to Facebook → Settings → Apps and Websites
  2. Find SocialBridge → Click "Remove"

Step 2: Request Manual Deletion

  1. Email: socialbridge@socialbridge.pt
  2. Subject: Data Deletion Request
  3. Include your User ID (from the bot)

Step 3: Confirmation

We delete all data within 30 days and confirm by email.
