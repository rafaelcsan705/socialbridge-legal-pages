Automate your social media posts from a single link

Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 31, 2025

SocialBridge does not store personal data. We only use temporary OAuth tokens to publish content on your Facebook and Instagram accounts, with your explicit permission.

1. What is SocialBridge?

SocialBridge is an automation tool that allows real estate agents and businesses to publish property listings on their Facebook Pages and Instagram Business accounts from a single URL. The user sends a property link → the system extracts images and description → publishes the post only if the user submits the URL.

2. Data We Collect

  • OAuth Access Tokens: Generated by Facebook/Instagram after login. Temporarily stored in encrypted Google Sheets to enable publishing.
  • Business Account Metadata: Instagram username, account ID, and Facebook Page ID — used only to confirm connection.
  • User-Submitted URLs: Property listing links (e.g., https://maisconsultores.pt/imovel/123) — processed in real-time for scraping.
  • No personal data: We do not access emails, private messages, friends, or non-business content.

3. How We Use the Data

  • Only to create and publish posts when the user sends a URL.
  • Activity logs (post ID, timestamp) are kept for debugging and support only.
  • Never used for advertising, resale, or user profiling.

4. Storage & Security

Tokens are stored in access-restricted Google Sheets and auto-expire every 60 days. URLs and scraped content are processed in real-time and not retained.

5. Your Rights (GDPR Compliant)

  • Revoke Access: Go to Facebook → Settings → Apps and Websites → Remove SocialBridge.
  • Request Deletion: Email socialbridge@socialbridge.pt with your user ID.
  • Response Time: All data deleted within 30 days.
